ZF18 Direct Power Coaching Phase 5

Zwift Fitness have once again teamed up with Direct Power Coaching in 2018, and will be bringing you some exciting new plans to kick off your fitness goals and develop your performance throughout the year.\n\nWe have broken the training into 10 phases. Each phase will take approximately 1-2 months to complete. It will consist of 3 workouts per week.\n\nThis next block is continuing the journey into strength & performance training.\n\nThe goals for this year is to lose unwanted pounds, develop a higher level of fitness and increase our threshold and performance.\n\nOur Coach: Pav Bryan\nBikes Etc Magazines Cycling Guru.
