Franklin Bespoke

Franklin Bespoke is a community led training environment based on Zwift.\n\nSet up to support the training aspirations of a number of athletes from around the world, the team at Franklin Bespoke have now opened their hive mind to the wider Zwift community. You can access any of their free workouts through their Facebook page, request bespoke training plans or workouts or simply ask any training based question. All of Franklin\'s workouts are based on percentage of FTP and therefore anybody of any ability is able to take part.\n\nMore recently Franklin have been working with both OneLove and TeamWKG to provide appropriate training solutions through both training plans and challenges. These vary from one off workouts up to 8 week training plans and race simulations. Find out more on the Franklin Bespoke Facebook page.
