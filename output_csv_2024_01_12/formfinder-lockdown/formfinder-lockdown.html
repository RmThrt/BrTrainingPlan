Formfinder Lockdown

Lockdown - maintain fitness\n\nPlan written by Jeremy Hunt, Formfinder.cc\n\nHere is a typical week of training for those looking to maintain reasonable fitness during lockdown. Once you get back to \'normal\' you should be able to spring back into great form without too much work.\n\n1 Week, to repeat:\nMonday: rest or light core gym or zone 1 (easy hour)\nTuesday: VO2 once per week\nWednesday: Gym work - follow strength program below\nThursday: 2x 18mins and 1x 20mins\nFriday: 60min zone 2 and 20min 90% with surges\nSaturday: Gym work\nSunday: 4x 5 SE at sub threshold heart rate + 1x 5min 110-120% FTP\n\nGym work: squats, calf raises, piston squats, press ups, sit ups, side plank, front plank, medicine ball balance
