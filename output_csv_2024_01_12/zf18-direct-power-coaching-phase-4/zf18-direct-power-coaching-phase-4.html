ZF18 Direct Power Coaching Phase 4

Zwift Fitness have once again teamed up with Direct Power Coaching in 2018, and will be bringing you some exciting new plans to kick off your fitness goals and develop your performance throughout the year.\n\nWe have broken the training into 9 phases. Each phase will take approximately 1-2 months to complete. It will consist of 3 workouts per week.\n\nThis next block is starting the journey into strength & performance training.\n\nThe goals for this year is to lose unwanted pounds, develop a higher level of fitness and increase our threshold and performance.\n\nOur Coach: Pav Bryan\nBikes Etc Magazines Cycling Guru. Director and Head Coach at Direct Power Coaching and Nutritionally Fit. Team Manager at Direct Power Cycling Team. Pioneer of Truly Personal Coaching and Truly Personal Nutrition.\n\nResponsible for a team of five coaches, Pav is a well-respected coach within the cycling community. Pav is experienced in public speaking and has presented numerous times around the world including being the 1066 Cycling Festivals Guest Speaker.\n\nPav loves donating his time to his local British Cycling Go-Ride club and nurturing the future of the sport. Pioneering the Truly Personal Coaching philosophy has seen his popularity among amateur athletes soar.\n\nPlease visit the DPC website for more information: directpowercoaching.com.\n\nJoin our community at facebook.com/groups/zwiftfitness/.
