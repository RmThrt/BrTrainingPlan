VO2max booster program

Workouts based on the VO2max Booster Program of Jesper Bondo Medhus, publicly available on www.training4cyclists.com/how-to-increase-your-vo2-max-in-14-days-vo2-max-test/. Zwift workout files created by Salvatore Iovene.\n\nThis training program will dramatically boost your race performance in only 14 days. The training program can be used by any cyclist, but riders with a power meter mounted on their bike will get the best value. Jesper mentions that there are amazing reports from readers who have tried this program. You can improve your VO2 max dramatically in only 14 days using these workouts.
