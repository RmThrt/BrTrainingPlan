Aionathletics

Hello, this is Martin, Trainer and Founder at Aionathletics.\n\nI want to present two free and pretty specific workouts for you enthusiastic Zwifters out there. The focus is on developing endurance strength or climbing! You\'ll get some nice muscular stimulus, but without producing too much lactate because of using your Slow Twitch fibers at 60-70RPM. You should get quick recovery because of this! This type of workout is best accompanied with real strength training like squats etc.!\n\nFrom time to time I will publish more workouts. These will be grouped for specific skills.\n\nFeel free to contact me and also make sure to visit www.aionathletics.com for plans and individualized training!\n\nMartin
