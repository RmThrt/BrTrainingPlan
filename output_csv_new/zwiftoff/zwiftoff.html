ZwiftOff Workouts created by Zee Kryder\n\nThe workouts are designed for weight loss. Based on the research of Dr. Jeukendrup, who identified the highest amount of fat is consumed at 45-52% of VO2Max or 50-68% of your given FTP. For the greatest benefit, a fasted early morning workout done in a strength gear with a cadence of 60-80 rpm will boost the benefits of the prescribed exercise. Your heart rate should fall into the 65-75% max heart rate.Try to breath deep and slow.\n\nThese workouts can also be used with a higher cadence for recovery or general fitness.\n\nJoin our Facebook group for support and our group workout schedule on Zwift: ZwiftOff Facebook Group\n\nRead my fitness and nutrition articles on Zwift Insider.
