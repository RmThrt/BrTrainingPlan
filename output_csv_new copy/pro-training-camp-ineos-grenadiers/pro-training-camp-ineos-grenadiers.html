This workout has a main set of 3 x 10 min threshold efforts, but to shake things up you will be doing the 1st and 10th minute at a higher effort.

    British professional cyclist, who currently rides for UCI World Team Ineos Grenadiers is part of a new generation of riders that competes in a variety of cycling disciplines.
